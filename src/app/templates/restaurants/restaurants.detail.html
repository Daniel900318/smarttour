Get an overview of this hotelNeo-classical luxury hotel with spa, in the heart of Helsinki

Opposite the park

Built in 1887 and still renowned for its superior service, this landmark neo-classical hotel stands in the heart of Helsinki across from Esplanade Park and 100 metres from the cathedral.

Spa, 3 restaurants

The 8th-floor Balance Club's spa offers aromatherapic mist showers, a mosaic Turkish bath, massage treatments, and a sun bed. The hotel also features a Japanese restaurant, a brasserie, and a lounge with park views.

Air conditioning

The 179 guestrooms at Hotel Kamp include marble bathrooms with heated floors, air conditioning, minibars, free WiFi, and satellite television.

Hotel Amenities

Hotel Amenities

Guests at Hotel Kamp can work out in the exercise room, or relax with a range of spa services including aromatherapy showers, heated relaxation beds, steam room, Turkish bath and traditional sauna. Massage services and a tanning room also available. The hotel also features three restaurants, each with its own special cuisine and ambience. Afternoon tea and pre-dinner drinks can be enjoyed in the bar and library. Guests can take advantage of butler, porter and bellhop services, and can contact the concierge desk for information on the local area. WiFi is free in public areas of the hotel, and there is onsite meeting rooms and a computer station. Those with cars can take advantage of valet parking, and a limousine service is available. There is a gift shop on site and practical amenities include currency exchange, laundry services and 24-hour front desk.

Where to Eat

Brasserie Kämp - Regional and international cuisine. Popular bar. Sunday brunch. Brasserie open daily, mid-day till late evening; bar open late morning till late evening.

Kämp Signé - Modern, fine-dining restaurant showcasing Finnish specialties. Focus on fresh Scandinavian seafood prepared with local wild herbs. Freshly baked spelt breads. European wines from artisan producers. View of Esplanade.

Yume - Japanese cuisine. Sushi, sashimi, tempura, and much more. Small plates for sharing. Seven-course omakase menu. Extensive sake list.

Kämp Upper Lounge - With Esplanade Park views, guests can sink into a comfortable chair and enjoy espresso drinks, teas, classic cocktails, aperitifs, and an extensive selection of fine wines by the glass. Private dining services are available.
